Cry Ankle
welcome to wusic.fm___________this Cry Ankle record was finished in the summer of 2024.
i've been hesitant to upload it to any major streaming service.
and i always dreamt of something like this, something ours.
i believe that as creators, we have the power to make something from nothing.
this is why I am excited to share my new project, wusic.fm
since this is a new project for me, i don't want to take unnecessary risks for a while.
that is why, for a few months you will only be able to see and hear my latest record,
"Apocalypse Forever?"
after a few months, i'll have a wider catalogue with amazing musicians.
stay tuned.
